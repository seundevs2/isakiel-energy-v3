USAGE NOTES
-----------
- For circular profile picture areas (Instagram, X), the battery container
  edges may get cropped. If you need a tighter circular crop, ask for a
  "mark-only" version (icons without the container).
- For dark backgrounds, ask for the inverted variant (cream container with
  dark icons).
- Always upload the LARGEST size a platform accepts — they downscale better
  than they upscale.
- Avoid JPG. PNG keeps the sharp edges of the lightning bolt.
